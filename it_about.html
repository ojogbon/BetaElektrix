WHO WE ARE

Beta Elektrix Limited is a specialist electro-mechanical engineering company driven by young and agile professionals, with clear focus on contracting, procurement, project management, consulting, training and outsourcing.

Our service offerings include design and manufacture of low voltage panels; supply, installation, commissioning and maintenance of medium voltage switchgears; calibration and testing services; process automation and industrial controls; building technology solutions and renewable energy.

Beta Elektrix also offers a robust training and consulting service covering the entire spectrum of industrial electro-mechanical systems.
